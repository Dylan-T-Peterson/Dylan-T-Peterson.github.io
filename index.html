About Me

Abstract

My name is Dylan Tyler Peterson, and I'm an Alaskan Native data analyst based in Anchorage, Alaska. My hobbies include crocheting, archery, lock picking, coding, hacking/pen testing, and programming. I'm a FOSS (Free and Open Source Software) enthusiast, with my main workstation running an Ubuntu distro of Linux (I aspire to overhaul to Gentoo eventually for performance and modular control reasons). I trained and am certified in data analytics through Thinkful.

Setup

My current work setup is an Asus ROG Strix laptop with an AMD Ryzen 9 5980HX CPU and an AMD Radeon RX 6800M GPU. I run Ubuntu as my primary work operating system. However, I run Windows on a secondary NVME SSD, mainly for gaming purposes and to familiarize myself with the OS and PowerShell. For my software setup on Ubuntu, I've replaced the standard GNOME Display Manager with I3WM, as tiled window managers are more intuitive and more streamlined to work with for me. I run most of my interactions through the command line.

FAQ

Q: Why are all of your SQL queries written with SQLite3 syntax?
A: I currently don't have the necessary infrastructure to obtain or maintain an actual server database, so instead of leaving out SQL as a whole, I decided to use the resources I currently have to practice and strengthen my SQL literacy.

Q: What is your development setup?
A: My main workstation is a Ubuntu-based setup running I3WM for my window manager. I've heavily customized a fork of Neovim, mainly for Python, SQL, and front-end web development. I modify my configuration files to improve productivity and ease of use when encountering things I could optimize during my projects.

Q: What professional experience do you have in data analytics?
A: I have a year of professional experience and another year of freelance experience. I keep my skills growing with my projects and continue to expand my understanding of analytical techniques and more efficient methods by staying informed and up to date-with new program/language releases.

Q: What experience do you have with analytic programs?
A: I have experience in Excel, Tableau, and creating custom Python scripts. My knowledge of Excel includes using HLOOKUP/VLOOKUP, INDEX/MATCH, and pivot charts/tables. In Tableau, I can create cohesive and insightful dashboards and stories. My most extensive area of expertise is manipulating and analyzing data through customized Python scripts and personalized SQL queries, including manipulating DataFrames through the Pandas module or database modification and querying through the SQLite3 module. I am proficient in creating data visualizations using Python's Matplotlib and Seaborn modules.

Q: What are your experiences with software development life cycle frameworks and processes?
A: I currently do not work on large enough projects or teams to necessitate an SDLC, so I need to gain experience with any SDLC framework. I have informed myself about various SDLC frameworks, including Scrum Agile, Waterfall, Lean, Iterative, and Spiral.
